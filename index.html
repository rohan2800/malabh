About the Film

An ageing man lives alone in a forest hut, far from the world he once knew. But every night, an unexpected visitor arrives, a man who knows him well. As long-buried fragments of the past begin to surface, a deep sense of guilt he has carried for years refuses to stay hidden.

Through breathtaking visuals and a deeply human narrative, Malabh asks a question we all carry

Mahadwar The Great Corridor

Mahadwar - the great Corridor is a Nagari Short film festival Golden Bioscope winner and people's choice award winning Documentary. It traces the fading soul of Mahadwar Road in Kolhapur, where memory and modern development collide. As redevelopment reshapes the space around the Mahalaxmi Temple, the film questions what is truly lost when a place disappears. In its echoes, the street still lives between memory, identity, and change.
